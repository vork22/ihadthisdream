Across nearly every tradition, water is the unconscious. Jung: 'Water is the commonest symbol for the unconscious.' Its state matters: clear water suggests clarity of feeling, turbid water a disturbance, tidal water cycles of emotion. In Vedic dream-lore, bathing in clean water is auspicious; drowning, a warning.
