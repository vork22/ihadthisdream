Freud associated tooth-loss with castration anxiety and repressed sexuality. Jung treated teeth as symbols of aggression and vitality — losing them signals a fear of losing one's bite in the world. In Chinese folk tradition, this dream is said to portend the illness or passing of an elder family member.
