Freud: phallic. Jung: the numinous, the kundalini, the ouroboros. In Hindu tradition, serpents (nāga) are guardians of thresholds and treasures. Many Indigenous North American traditions regard snake dreams as signaling seasonal or spiritual transition — a shedding of an old skin.
