In many Indigenous cosmologies (notably Diné and Hopi) Spider Grandmother is a creator figure; in Greek myth, Arachne is hubris punished. Freudian readings often involve the mother; Jung, the feminine principle in its weaving aspect. A web you are caught in asks: who spun this?
