The tower concentrates vertical aspiration into cut stone—or steel—or impossibly shimmering dream-substance refusing Newton kindly. Flood myths drown horizons; towers pierce clouds. Across depth-psychological and cartomantic vocabularies, tower dreams often stage **how psyche handles height**: earned perspective, walled altitude, brittle magnificence shattered by unforeseen voltage.

Tower ≠ mountain: mountains grow over geologic lyric; towers accrete deliberate lifts. Anthropological dream readers note engineered height frequently tracks **credentialised identity scaffolding** separating dreamer socially from ancestral ground—even when geography flat.

## Biblical undertow

Genesis' Babel cluster narrates multilingual fracture after collective attempt to unify speech under commanding vertical city project. Psychological reading (non-pneumatically coercive) surfaces anxiety that **singular narrative control collapses**: inner parliament voices refuse harmonised marching orders. Dreams re-stage incomplete translation between parts.

Lightning here is thematic cousin to Tarot's bolt—interruption deity or psyche dispatches restructuring without committee approval.

Tarot Marseilles lineage's *Maison Dieu* (vernacular Tower) shows crowned figures expelled mid-air—privileged fictions humbled. Psychological tarot interpreters emphasise liberation cost: shock first, panorama second.

Dreamers without tarot familiarity still reproduce lightning-strike facades spontaneously—cultural saturation isn't prerequisite; archetypal condensation suffices per Jung CW 9i discussion of spontaneously recurring geometries & mythic condensation.

## Jungian registers

Lightning here parallels **solve** corrosion stage—not punishment theatre but chemical necessity before *coagula* cohesion returns.
