The door is a threshold guarded by Janus in Roman tradition — the god of beginnings who faces both directions at once. Dream-doors are decisions rendered visual. A locked door asks who holds the key in waking life; an open door invites but does not compel; a door you cannot find, a transition you are not ready to see. Many dreamers report endless corridors of doors — a dream often associated with decision fatigue or identity confusion. Notice which door you choose, and whether you hesitate. The dream is less interested in what is behind each door than in what drew you to one over another.
