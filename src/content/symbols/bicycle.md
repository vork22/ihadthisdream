The bicycle is self-powered motion that requires balance. Jungian analysis reads bicycle-dreams as the ego moving forward under its own effort — sustainable, human-scaled, but requiring continued attention. A bicycle that loses its wheel often marks a waking effort that has become untenable without help. Notice whether the hill goes up or down, and whether you ride alone.
